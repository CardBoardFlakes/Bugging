Closure

While Bug types are strong against certain other types, they are overall severely underpowered. This is especially apparent through their DPS, average stats, and matchups. Furthermore the top bug types such as Mega Heracross, Mega Pinsir, and Mega Scizor are mega pokemon which you can only have one at a time, this is furthered by the unavalability of mega energy as it takes a while for them to return. This trend of exclusivity is also reflected by the other top DPS options like Shadow Regigigas requiring hidden power bug and Volcarona requiring 400 candy to evolve and being an rare egg exclusive. However despite these flaws bug types are still very good against dark types and they perform great in PvP. Pokemon Go developers should ensure that all traits are created and maintained equally to provide balance and diversity within the meta.
